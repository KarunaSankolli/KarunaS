YourLiitleShop.Goa is an online thrift shop on instagram and facebook created for second hand shopping.It also serves as a awareness platform for waste management

Story

It all started when during lock down we three friends came together to create a online thrift store experience for Goans as we saw the growing popularity ofthriftstores and the lack of it in our State. Sooner than later it grew from eing just another thriftstore to a awareness genarating platform for waste mnagement as well as shopping!The idea has always been to help establish a legible platform for those who want to sell and buy second as it helps reduce the demand of clothing and makes waste management easier.
